# Prisoner's Dilemma — Reciprocity

Reciprocity analysis for the prisoners calibration study (mechanisms **M**, **MP**,
**IM**, **IJM**, **IMP**, **IJMPQ**, and related). Partner-choice analysis is
documented in **prisoners_partner_choice.md**.

## Status

Simulations for the prisoners study under the current parameterization are still
running. No reciprocity analysis is available yet.

When data are complete, this document will cover:

- Direct reciprocity (M): cooperation profiles across the (R, P) grid, shuffle
  vs noshuffle contrast
- Combined mechanisms (MP, MPQ, IM, IJM, IMP, IJMPQ): mechanism comparisons
  across pop_1, pop_2, and pop_3
- Shuffle effects on reciprocity-based mechanisms
- Cross-study comparison with Hamilton reciprocity patterns

See **prisoners.md** for study status and **hamilton_reciprocity.md** for
reciprocity patterns on the equal-cost diagonal while prisoners data are pending.
